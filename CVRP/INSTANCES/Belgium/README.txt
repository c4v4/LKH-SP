Capacitated vehicle routing problem problem (CVRP)

The subdirectory INSTANCES contains the following benchmark instances:

	Augerat_A (27 instances)
	Augerat_B (23 instances)
	Augerat_P (24 instances)
	Source:
	Augerat, P., Belenguer, J. M., Benavent, E., Corberin, A., Naddef, D.:
	Separating capacity constraints in the CVRP using tabu search.
	Eur. J. Oper. Res., 106(2-3):546-557 (1998)

	Belgium (10 instances)
	Source:
	Arnold, F., Gendreau, M., Sörensen, K.:
	Efficiently solving very large-scale routing problems.
	Comput. Oper. Res., 107:32-42 (2019)
	
	Breedam (8 instances)
	Breedam, A. V.:
	An Analysis of the Behavior of Heuristics for the Vehicle Routing Problem 
	for a selection of problems with Vehicle-related, Customer-related, and 	
	Time-related Constraints.
	PhD thesis, University of Antwerp (1994)

	CMT (14 instances)
	Source:
	Christofides, N., Mingozzi, A., Toth, P.:
	The vehicle routing problem.
	In Christofides, N., Mingozzi, A., Toth, P., Sandi, C. (Eds.):
	Combinatorial optimization: vol. 1, pp. 315–338, (1979).

	Eilon (15 instances)
	Source:	
	Christofides, N., Eilon, S.:
	An algorithm for the vehicle-dispatching problem. 
	Oper. Res. Quart., 20:309–318 (1969)

	Fishetti (32 asymmetric instances)
	Source:
	Fischetti, M., Toth, P.:
	A new ILP-based refinement heuristic for Vehicle Routing Problems.
	Math. Prog., 105(2):471-499 (2006)
	
	Fisher (3 instances)
	Source:
	Fisher, M. L., Jaikumar, R.:
	A generalized assignment heuristic for vehicle routing.
	Networks, 11(3): 109-124 (1981).

	Golden (21 instances)
	Source:
	Golden, B. L, Magnanti, T. L., Nguen, H. Q.:
	Implementing vehicle routing algorithms.
	Networks, 7(2):113-148 (1977)

	Kytojoki (20 instances)
	Sources:
	Kytöjoki, J., Nuortio, T., Bräysy, O., Gendreau, M.:
	An efficient variable neighborhood search heuristic for very large scale
	vehicle routing problems.
	Comput. Oper. Res., 34(3):2743-2757 (2007)

	Arnold, F, Gendreau, Sörensen, K.:
	Efficiently Solving Very large Scale Routing Problems.
	CIRRELT-2017-75, 2017.

	LG (120 instances)
	Source:
	Letchford, A., Salazar-González.J.-J.:
	Stronger multi-commodity flow formulations of the Capacitated 
	Vehicle Routing Problem.
	Eur. J. Oper. Res., 244(3):730-738 (2015)

	Li (12 instances)
	Source:
	Li, F., Golden B., and Wasil, E.:
	Very large-scale vehicle routing: new test problems, algorithms, and
	results.
	Comput. Oper. Res., 32(5):1165-1179 (2005)

	M (5 instances)
	Source:
	Christofides, N., Mingozzi, A., Toth, P.:
	The vehicle routing problem.
	In Christofides, N., Mingozzi, A., Toth, P., Sandi, C. (Eds.):
	Combinatorial optimization: vol. 1, pp. 315–338, (1979).

	R (67 instances) EXACT_2D
	Source:
	Christofides, N., Eilon, S.:
	An algorithm for the vehicle-dispatching problem. 
	Oper. Res. Quart., 20:309–318 (1969)

	S (73 instances)
	Source:	
	Christofides, N., Eilon, S.:
	An algorithm for the vehicle-dispatching problem. 
	Oper. Res. Quart., 20:309–318 (1969)

	Taillard (13 instances)
	Source:
	Rochat, Y., Taillard, É. D.:
	Probabilistic diversification and intensification in local
	search for vehicle routing.
	J. Heur., 1(1): 147-167 (1995)

	TSPLIB (13 instances)
	Source:
	Gröer, C.:
	Parallel and serial algorithms for vehicle routing problems.
	PhD thesis, University of Maryland (2008)

	U (41 instances) EXACT_2D
	Collection of Augerat, Christofides, and Fisher instances.

	Uchoa (100 instances)
	Source:
	Uchoa, E., Pecin, D., Pessoa, A., Poggi, M., Vidal, T., and 
	Subramanian, A.:
	New Benchmark Instances for the Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem.
	Eur. J. Oper. Research, 257(3):845-858 (2017)

	Z (36 instances)
	Large scale instances proposed by Golden et al., Li et al., and
	Zachariais and Kiranoudis.
